We process your data on the basis of your consent and our legitimate interest in responding to enquiries and running our business. You can withdraw consent at any time.

We only share data with trusted providers who help us operate (such as hosting and email), and only as needed. We keep your information no longer than necessary for the purpose it was collected.

Your rights

Under UK GDPR you can ask to access, correct or delete your personal data, or object to how we use it. Email us and we'll action it promptly. You can also complain to the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO) at ico.org.uk.

Cookies

We use only essential cookies and privacy-respecting analytics. We don't use intrusive advertising trackers.
