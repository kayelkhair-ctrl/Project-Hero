What is GEO, and how is it different from SEO?

SEO (Search Engine Optimisation) helps you rank on Google. GEO (Generative Engine Optimisation) helps you get found and cited inside AI answers from tools like ChatGPT, Gemini and Perplexity. We work on both so you stay visible as search changes.
